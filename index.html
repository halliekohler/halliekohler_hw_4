Growing up in northern Michigan, Hallie found inspiration in the lakes and pines. She began documenting the scenery at an early age, family and friends the first stars of her photographic chronicles.

As a studio art major, Hallie studied photography, drawing, and printmaking at Colgate University in Hamilton, New York. Her breadth of knowledge in multiple mediums shaped her eclectic artistic sense. In Florence, Italy, Hallie studied at Studio Art Centers International, developing her keen sense of absorbing what matters and what's interesting -- with a instinctive desire to record it all. She has traveled around the U.S. and Europe to shoot and sketch.

Hallie's passion for capturing experiences is rivaled only by her thirst for adventure and desire that her photographs and videos draw people closer to the places and people she encounters.

She currently lives and works in Brooklyn, New York, as a freelance Photographer, Videographer, Designer, and Maker of Things. Odd jobs and multi-tasking abound.
